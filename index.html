About Scott

Professional triathlete and endurance coach based in Beaverton, Oregon

My name is Scott Ludford, and I am a Professional Triathlete based in Beaverton, Oregon. I have been coaching athletes throughout North America and Europe for over 4 years in all race formats (draft-legal/non-draft-legal Sprint, Olympic, 70.3, and Full Ironman distances), working with athletes of all levels to develop a specialized training program and achieve their goals.

As a coach, my focus is to deliver unique, high-quality training programs that are customized to each athlete's goals, schedule, and lifestyle. My coaching philosophy emphasizes a person centered approach—integrating individualized endurance training, nutrition planning, and strength work, while creating a plan that is structured around your responsibilities outside of training.

I work closely with a wide range of athletes, from those training for their first triathlon, to elite age-group competitors and aspiring professionals, providing both online and in-person coaching designed to help each athlete reach their full potential.

My Coaching Philosophy

Individual Focus

Every athlete receives a training plan tailored specifically to their unique physiology, goals, and lifestyle. No templates. No shortcuts.

Data-Driven

We use performance data to refine your training continuously. Regular communication ensures you're always on track to meet your goals.

Holistic Approach

Training extends beyond workouts. We consider nutrition, recovery, mental preparation, and life balance to optimize your performance.

Your Journey

Whether you're an elite athlete or new to endurance sports, I'll help you push your boundaries and achieve what you didn't think was possible.
